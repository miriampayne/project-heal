What is Project Heal?

In today's modern world there is an ever increasing need to be constantly switched on. The pressure to multi-task is wearing down our attention capacity and ability to be mindful as we force our brain to switch from one task to another throughout our day.

Project Heal is a roadmap to heal and transform how you feel. You will get self-care practices straight to your inbox, all designed to rebuild your energy so that you can become more attuned to your body and avoid the all too familiar anxiety, depletion and burn-out.

There is a path of living which is nourishing and restorative, rather than draining and forceful which allows you to flow with life rather than against it. We have an ethos of shared knowledge and research that underpins our mission, and a commitment to promoting goals of holistic health, creating a community that encompasses a collective endeavour of mental awareness at local, national and global levels.
